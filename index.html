Who Are We?


We're a group of high-achieving students from the top high schools and programs around Canada. We want to share our knowledge and experiences with younger students, and help them excel in both their academic courses at school and skills outside the classroom.

We offer curriculum courses like math, science, and french, as well as skill courses such as debate, game development, coding, and visual arts. All of our tutors have an academic grade higher than an A (90) and are part of programs like the International Baccalaureate (IB), Gifted, TOPS, MACS, Advanced Placement (AP) and more.

Why Choose Us?

Some traits about our tutoring and organization make us unique - we combine both our experience and skills to craft unique sessions to maximize learning and interest.

Affordability

Our sessions are affordable because we understand how money can be a challenge for families. Our goal is helping students and fundraising for education, not profit.

Specialization

Tutors are experienced in the subject they teach. They've won outstanding awards, achievements and some have represented the country in competitions of that subject.

Relatability

We have passion and believe in performace-oriented learning. Our approach to teaching is unique as we're also students and know how some concepts can be taught more effectively.

Why did we start this?


Around the world and also in Canada, many students in certain communities do not have access to proper education, like new immigrants, the lower class, and indigenous communities. Our goal is to raise money for those students and provide them with resources and opportunities.

Especially during these difficult times, many students are not able to receive proper education due to a variety of reasons. We want to change that. Education should be accessible to everyone.
