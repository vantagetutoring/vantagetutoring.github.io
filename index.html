Who Are We?


We're a group of high-achieving high school students in the GTA who wish to make education more equitable in our community.

We believe everyone should be given the opportunity to have access to high quality education. Our goal as a not-for-profit is to make learning more accessible for all students in our community. We connect students with high school or university tutors in the GTA region, provide opportunities for volunteers, and implement educational fundraising for schools in need of support.

Why did we start this?


Around the world and specifically in Canada, there are many students who do not have access to proper educational resources. Communities such as the indenous, new immigrants, and low-income families are most heavily impacted. Our goal is to fundraise and support these communities and provide those in need with resources and opportunities.

Especially during these difficult times, many students cannot recieve proper education due to a variety of reasons. We want to change that. Education is for everyone.
