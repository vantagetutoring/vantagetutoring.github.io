Tutoring Classes

$10 - $20 per hour

  • Tutors have excelled in a specific subject area at school (above 95%) and have won in compeitions in the field.
  • Classes are divided into curriculum and contest tutoring. Group classes are available for cheaper rates but are limited to a maximum of 4 students.
  • Classes are 1 hour long and span a term of 8 classes.
  • We begin with a free trial class where the tutor will assess the students' skill. Along with suggestions from the student and parent, we will create a personal course using our resources for the student.

Workshops and Webinars

Free

  • We host free workshops ranging from raising awareness about education to high school mentoring sessions.
  • We arrange events throughout the school year for both students and parents.

Questions and Answers

Are tutoring sessions / workshops physical or virtual?

All of our activities are currently virtual due to the pandemic. We are using platforms like Google Meet, Zoom, and other messaging services to host our classes and events.

Private and Group Classes

Lessons can be made private or group upon availability or request. We try to match students up with other students of the same level and subject when available.

Rates on charts are for private lessons. Grouped lessons are cheaper and the maximum student per group is 2-4 depending on subject. Grouped lessons start at a rate of $8.5 per student however vary depending on student count and subject.
