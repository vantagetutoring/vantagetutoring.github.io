Our Vision

We believe everyone should be given the opporunity to have access to high quality education. Our goal is to create a local and global community where all students have a chance at education, regardless their location, background, or family circumstances.

What do we do?

At Vantage, we tutor and host events to empower students, raise awareness, and fundraise to support students who lack resources to learn. Our activities include tutoring, events, and fundraisers to support low-income or students in minority groups.

We'd love to partner with schools or similar organizations! We would love to run one-off events like workshops or seminars, or even weekly sessions spanning a whole school term!
