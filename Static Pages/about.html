Our Vision

We believe everyone should be given the opportunity to have access to high quality education. Our goal as a not-for-profit is to make learning more accessible for all GTA students. By connecting students with high school or university tutors in the GTA region and providing opportunities for volunteers, we strive to implement educational fundraising for learning communities in need of support.

What do we do?

At Vantage, we tutor and host events to empower students, raise awareness, and fundraise to support students who lack resources to learn. Our activities include tutoring, events, and fundraisers to support low-income or students in minority groups.

Partnership

We are currently interested in partnering with schools and other education organizations. We would love to run events like workshops, seminars, or semester-long weekly tutoring sessions. All of our members and organizers are high school volunteers who have a passion to learn and teach! Email us at tutoring.vantage@gmail.com if you are interested or have questions.
